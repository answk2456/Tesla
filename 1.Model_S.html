What is Model S?

The Model S is a five-door liftback sedan.
Deliveries began on June 22, 2012.As of April 2020, the Model S has two configurations: the Model S Long Range Plus, and the Model S Performance with EPA ranges of 391 miles (629 km) and 348 miles (560 km), respectively.
