What is Model Y?

The Model Y is compact crossover utility vehicle.
The Model Y is built on a platform that shares many components with the Model 3. The car has up to three rows of seats (up to 7 people), 66 cu ft (2 m3) of cargo space with the second and third rows folded, and will have a range of up to 300 miles (480 km).
