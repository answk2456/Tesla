What is Model X?

The Tesla Model X is a mid-size crossover SUV with a lightweight aluminum body.
Model X deliveries started in September 2015.[231] It is offered in 5-, 6- and 7-passenger configurations. The passenger doors are articulating "falcon-wing" designs that open vertically.
