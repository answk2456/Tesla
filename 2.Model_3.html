What is Model 3?

The Model 3 is a four-door fastback sedan. Tesla unveiled the Model 3 on March 31, 2016.
Potential customers began to reserve spots on March 31 with a refundable deposit. One week after the unveiling, Tesla reported over 325,000 reservations.
Bloomberg News claimed "the Model 3's unveiling was unique in the 100-year history of the mass-market automobile."
